🎨 Notes

  • VO recording: record full narration track FIRST in quiet room with good audio (use phone voice memos or USB mic). Narrator should speak naturally, not rushed — match the 01:21 pacing.
  • B-roll: film all visuals silently at 24 or 30fps. Editor will sync VO to shot numbers.
  • Batch filming: shoot all bathroom shots in one session, all kitchen in another, etc. (see Hero Shot List Group column).
  • Captions: editor will add animated captions in post using VO transcript — talent does NOT need to say words on camera in sync.
  • Natural light: shoot near windows during daytime for best quality (avoid harsh direct sun on faces — use sheer curtains or shoot in shade).
  • Clear skin close-ups: use good even lighting, slight diffusion (sheer curtain or bounce light) to show realistic texture without harsh shadows — we want authentic, not retouched.
  • Product shots: keep backgrounds clean/uncluttered. Blue bottle should pop against neutral or green/white backgrounds.
